Types of Candy

Hard Candy

A hard candy is a sugar candy prepared from one or more sugar-based syrups that are boiled and then cooled to harden.

Examples:

  • Jolly Ranchers
  • Root Beer Barrels
  • Peppermints

Chocolates

Chocolate is a food made from roasted ground cacao beans.

Examples:

  • M&Ms
  • Hershey Bars
  • Milky Way

Gum

A preparation usually made of sweeted chicle for chewing.

Examples:

  • Chiclets
  • Juicy Fruit
  • Bazooka
